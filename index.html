Welcome to my webpage! I was born in the north of Iran, Gilan province, where you see the pictures here. Gilan province contains sandy Caspian Sea beach and also green mountains as you see in my pictures. I am currently Associate Professor at Xinjiang Astronomical Observatory(XAO) and Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS), Urumqi, China. I was postdoctoral researcher at the School of Astronomy, Institute for research in Fundamental Sciences (also known as IPM), Tehran, Iran. Also, I won fellowship of Center for International Science and Technology Interactions and a separate research grant for 14 months during my postdoctoral period at IPM. I was working in group of Prof Fatemeh Tabatabaei. I am simulating radio data for SKA. Before this position, I was postdoctoral fellow at National Astronomical Observatories of China and Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China, with fellowship from China postdoctoral foundation. I worked in group of Prof Youjun Lu at NAOC/CAS. For my PhD, I studied at Department of Physics of Fudan University, Shanghai, China with CSC scholarship under supervision of Prof Cosimo Bambi. My research was mainly about testing general relativity with electromagnetic radiation. I also was postgraduate diploma student of The Abdus Salam ICTP, Trieste, Italy, after my master studies. I studied the cosmological dark matter power spectrum as a small project under supervision of Prof Paolo Creminelli. My master and bachelor studies were in Tehran and Zanjan city of Iran, respectively. My master thesis was under supervision of Prof Hassan Firouzjahi about the methods of delta-N formalism in models of cosmic inflation and non-Gaussian perturbations.
